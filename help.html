so the boxes are just rules, with the check boxes saying how many neighbors the cell has. This uses a Moore neighborhood, if you didn't read that far, it's ok, it just means that a cell's neighbors are all of the ones directly touching it, like minesweeper, although I plan to make this changable in the future.

still doesn't make sense? I don't blame you, lets go a little deeper

The biggest box is the color of the cell, the smaller boxes inside of them are the color they can change into, and the smallest boxes are how many of that neighbor it will take to change them.

A white cell will never change to a white cell

A white cell will change to a black cell if it has 3 black neighbors

A black cell will change into a white cell if it has 0,1,4,5,6,7,8 black neighbors

A black cell will never change into a black cell
